This Privacy Policy describes how Individual Entrepreneur Kotov Sergei (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, processes, and discloses your information in connection with our mobile application(s) distributed through Huawei AppGallery (hereinafter referred to as the "App").

The App is a single-player offline game that does not require a constant internet connection for its core functionality.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

2.1. Information Collected Automatically

2.2. Payment Information

All in-app purchases are processed exclusively through Huawei AppGallery's payment system. We do not collect, process, or store any payment card details, bank account information, or other financial data. All payment transactions are handled securely by Huawei in accordance with their privacy policy.

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:
